Accuracy — 100-question benchmarks

Pass rate across reasoning/coding/knowledge suites. Phase 1 complete (3 models × 6 benches). GPQA truncation caveat: 27b had 15/100 and 35b-a3b had 23/100 hard-capped at max_tokens=32 768 (counted as wrong); true GPQA likely 78–85 % and 75–83 % respectively — see results/M4_MAX_128GB_NOTES.md.

Local vs frontier — published scores

Local runs (this rig, n=100, seed=42, temp=0) plotted against published first-party scores for frontier & notable open-weight models. Caveats: many newer frontier models no longer publish HumanEval/DROP; MATH often means MATH-500 or AIME 2025 (noted per-model); GPQA is Diamond throughout. Reasoning models (o3, R1, Claude 4.x with thinking, Gemini 2.5/3, GPT-5) report with test-time compute. Use for ranking, not absolute deltas — ±5pp noise at n=100.
